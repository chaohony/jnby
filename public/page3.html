EVERYONE'S BIZARRE

Maybe you are like square pegs in round holes at school, or maybe you are the shortest, the fattest, or the weirdest in the school.

spring/summer

2018

The point is what you do when you're alone. Maybe you are assembling the computers, or practicing cello, or playing rock. Actually, the things that you are really doing make you get more and more interesting. Someday, when other people notice you, they will find you are cooler than they have imagined before.

So WHAT? IT DOES NOT MATTER.
