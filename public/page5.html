2016年7月，江南布衣集团推出了“Z世代”品牌“蓬马”（Pomme de terre）。品牌展现了一个随性、鬼马、雅痞的形象。设计注重质感的呈现，寻求轻松的着装体验与更深层次的情感触动。并且填补了青少年设计师品牌这一市场空缺。 处在“自我发展期”的Z世代群体，有自主意识，自信心和价值观的养成充分影响着他们的未来。蓬马作为这个群体的发言人，从衣食住行多个维度，给予他们足够的探索空间，找到一种更适合他们自由成长的生活方式。

In July 2016, JNBY Group launched the “Generation Z” brand “Pomme de terre ”that shows a youth image who are individual, wicked and enjoyable. The design focuses on the presentation of the texture, seeking for a relaxed dressing experience and a deeper emotional touch. It also fills the market gap of young designer brand. The young people of “Generation Z” in the “self exploration period” have a strong sense of independent consciousness. The cultivation of self-confidence and values will fully affect their future. As a spokesperson for young people, Pomme de terre gives them enough space to find a way of life which is more suitable for them to grow freely in many dimensions of clothing, food, living and outing.

Z世代：意指在1990年代中期至2000年出生的人。他们又被称为网络世代、互联网世代，统指受到互联网、即时通讯、短讯、MP3、智能手机和平板电脑等科技产物影响很大的一代人。

Generation Z：it refers to the people who born between the mid-1990s and 2000s. They are also known as the Net Generation or the Internet Generation, which is a generation that has been influenced by technology products such as the Internet, instant messaging, SMS, MP3, smart-phone and IPAD.
